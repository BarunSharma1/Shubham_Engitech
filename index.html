About Us

The Intelligent Way
to Plan

The Shubham Engitech Pvt Ltd construction company was established in 2014. Mr. Vilas B. Jamdar is the Director of the company. We are a general contractor offering construction services in terms of Site Analysis, Feasibility Studies, Preliminary Design Studies, and Pre-engineering Structures.

We handle various industrial distribution, manufacturing office, retail, recreational and commercial projects in Chakan MIDC Area, Dist-Pune. We have successfully completed construction of 45+ Industrial Sheds with office buildings in the Chakan MIDC Area.

Shubham Engitech is also a leading company in Plot Trading services. We have sold a total of 1000+ MIDC plots in the last 12 years in Chakan MIDC Ph-I to Ph-V and Talegaon MIDC, Pune — including plot trading with foreign companies such as Sav Chemicals Private Limited (Taiwan) and Andreas STIHL Private Limited.
